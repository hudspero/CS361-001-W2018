How do I change my ONID password?
How do I set up/change my alternate contact information?
  1. Go to onid.oregonstate.edu.
  2. On the top left of the page, click "Change Password".
  3. If you do know your current ONID password, select the first option and follow the directions on-screen.
  4. If you do not know your current ONID password, select the second option. You will be prompted to enter your OSU ID number, birth date, first name, and last name.
  5. You will be prompted to send a Password Reset PIN to a phone number and alternate email. If you have no information displayed, you will need to contact the Office of the Registrar. (541) 737-4331
  6. When you receive the PIN, enter it and click Submit.
When creating a new password, it must meet the following categories:
  • Cannot exactly match any previous passwords.
  • Cannot exactly match a word found in the English dictionary.
  • Must be at least eight (8) characters long.
  • Must contain one (1) uppercase letter, one (1) lowercase letter, and one (1) number.
If you have difficulty changing your ONID password, call either (541) 737-8787 or (541) 737-4331.

Personal Printers
Because personal printers can be a network security liability, we strongly recommend that you connect to a personal printer by a USB cable if you use a personal printer on-campus.
  • Is the printer currently plugged in and turned on?
  • Have you tried turning your printer off and on? Unplugging and replugging the cable?
  • Is your computer notifying you that a connection could not be made?
If you're still having difficulty, contact the printer's manufacturer or call (541) 737-8787.

For Windows (Windows 7 and above):
  1. Click the network icon in the bottom right.


  2. Click on the network you wish to connect to.


  3. Click Connect or double-click on the name.
For Macintosh (10.10 Yosemite and above):
  1. Click on the Apple icon in the top left.
  2. Select System Preferences.


  3. Go to Network


  4. Click on the network you wish to connect to.

If you need to enter a username and password, you will be prompted to enter them. Your device will tell you if you are connected, or if a connection cannot be established. If you need further help, please contact the IS Service Desk at (541) 737-8787.
